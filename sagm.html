Semi-Annual General Meeting

Saturday, November 17, 2012
Bethany UCC directions
6:00 p.m.

This is official notice, pursuant to the Constitution and by-laws of the Chicago Branch of the Royal Scottish Country Dance Society, that the Annual General Meeting will be held on Saturday, November 17. The meeting will be held at Bethany UCC, starting at 6:00 p.m. The main items on the agenda are approval of changes to the Constitution and By-Laws and approval of the 2011-2012 Treasurer's Report. Anyone who is interested is invited to attend, but you must be a member of the Branch to vote.

The SAGM will be followed by the Fall Dance (see below).
