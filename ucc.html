Directions to the Ukrainian Cultural Center

From the west: Take the Eisenhower Expressway (I-290) eastbound to Western Ave. (2400 W) and turn left to go north on Western. Drive about 1 1/4 miles to Chicago Ave. (800 N) and turn right onto Chicago. Go one block to Oakley and turn right to enter the parking lot.

From the south, east, or north: From I-90/94 (Kennedy or Dan Ryan) go west on the Eisenhower Expressway to Damen Ave. (2000 W) exit. Turn right onto Damen and drive about 1 1/4 miles north to Chicago Ave. (800 N) and turn left onto Chicago. Go about a half mile to Oakley and turn left on Oakley to enter the parking lot.

There is a small parking lot on the left and a large parking lot further down the alley on the right (see parking map)
