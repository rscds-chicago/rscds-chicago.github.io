If you enjoy Scottish Country Dancing, please become a MEMBER of the Royal Scottish Country Dance Society—Chicago Branch

Support and promote Scottish country dancing and culture both in our local area and throughout the world!

My RSCDS membership...

  • Trains dance teachers and musicians
  • Publishes books of dances, traditional and modern
  • Publishes sound recordings of music for dancing
  • Supports the RSDCS Chicago website that provides news about local and regional dance events
  • Allows me to participate in Branch and Society management

Membership includes...

  • a subscription to the Scottish Country Dancer magazine -- direct from Edinburgh -- with news, dance instructions, and places to dance world-wide
  • a 10% discount on most RSCDS recordinga and publications
  • Branch voting privileges

How much does it cost?

  • Full RSCDS membership for one year (Sept - Aug) is $45
    • There is a discount for 2 adults in one household - 2 for $70
  • Young adults 18-24 and full-time students pay only $32 per year
  • Youth ages 12-17 are $20 per year
    • (We no longer offer the “Mailing” membership category, but if you provide an email address, we are happy to send notifications electronically at no charge)
