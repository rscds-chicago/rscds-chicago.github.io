Directions to the Irish American Heritage Center

The center is located at 4626 N. Knox Avenue in Chicago and is about seven miles north of downtown Chicago. The center requests that we do not park on the street. A free lot is just North of the Center.

Northbound on Kennedy Expressway (90/94): At split of 90 and 94 take 94 north and immediately take Wilson Avenue exit and go one block East. The Heritage Center is located on the left. Turn left on Knox and left into parking lot.

Southbound Kennedy Expressway (90): Take Lawrence Avenue exit, turn left, and take a right on Kilpatrick Avenue. The Heritage Center is a couple of blocks down on the left.

Southbound on Edens Expressway (94): Take Wilson Avenue exit. Take a left and go one block. The Heritage Center is located on the left. Turn left on Knox and left into parking lot.

CTA directions: Take the blue line L train to the Montrose station. Go East on Montrose to Kilbourn. Take a left on Kilbourn (North) to Wilson Avenue. Take a left on Wilson (West) to Knox Avenue. Take a right on Knox (North) to the Heritage Center.
