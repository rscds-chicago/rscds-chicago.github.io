Annual General Meeting

Sunday, June 10, 2012
Bethany UCC
6:00 p.m.

This is official notice, pursuant to the Constitution and by-laws of the Chicago Branch of the Royal Scottish Country Dance Society, that the Annual General Meeting will be held on Sunday, June 10. The meeting will be held at Bethany UCC, starting at 6:00 p.m. The main item on the agenda will be the election of a new Committee of Management. Anyone who is interested is invited to attend, but you must be a member of the Branch to vote.

The AGM will be followed by the Spring Dance (see below).
